Want to ride? Here's how

To ride the park you must become members of skaterham. Membership is free.

To become members you need follow the link to Session bookings and register there.

You can book your session and become members at the same time if you want to. Don't forget that membership is free

When you first register online through our booking system you will fill in a questionnaire and consent form which give you membership.
